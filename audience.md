<section id="audience">
    <h2>Target Audience</h2>
    <p>This workshop is relevant to a broad set of communities working on open government, public access, and electronic discovery, including:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>The open government community</li>
        <li>FOIA requesters, including investigative journalists and civil society organisations who request information under public disclosure laws in various jurisdictions</li>
        <li>The legal community interested in public records discovery and disclosure obligations</li>
        <li>Privacy and data protection advocates, particularly those focused on GDPR-aligned sensitivity review</li>
        <li>Government agencies and archival institutions working on information management and disclosure workflows</li>
        <li>AI researchers working on information retrieval and natural language processing</li>
        <li>The intelligence and declassification community, to the extent tools and techniques discussed have relevance to declassification of government records</li>
    </ul>
</section>
