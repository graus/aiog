    <h2>Submission Guidelines</h2>
    <p>We invite submissions of:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Research papers</strong> – original research contributions</li>
        <li><strong>Position papers</strong> – insights from practice</li>
    </ul>
    <p>All submissions should be formatted in the <a href="https://ceurws.wordpress.com/2020/03/31/ceurws-publishes-ceurart-paper-style/" target="_blank">CEUR workshop proceedings template</a> and submitted via EasyChair (link TBA).</p>
    <p>Submissions will be peer-reviewed by the program committee. Accepted papers will be published in CEUR Workshop Proceedings.</p>
