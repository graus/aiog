## Submission Guidelines

We invite submissions of:

- **Research papers** – original research contributions
- **Position papers** – insights from practice

All submissions should be formatted in the [CEUR workshop proceedings template](https://ceurws.wordpress.com/2020/03/31/ceurws-publishes-ceurart-paper-style/) and submitted via EasyChair (link TBA).

Submissions will be peer-reviewed by the program committee. Accepted papers will be published in CEUR Workshop Proceedings.
